Nanjing, 210023, China
Email: zhangyuan AT nju.edu.cn

I am a research member of COSEC, led by Prof. Sheng Zhong. I received B.S. (2005) in Automation from Tianjin University, Ms.E. (2009) in Software Engineering from Tsinghua University, and Ph.D. (2013) in Computer Science from State University of New York at Buffalo. Currently I am a professor and a Ph.D. advisor in the School of Computer Science at Nanjing University.

My research interests include security, privacy, and economic incentives.
