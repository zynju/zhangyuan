Yuan Zhang

Associate Professor
Computer Science and Technology Department
Nanjing University
Nanjing, 210023, China

I am a research member of COSEC, led by Prof. Sheng Zhong. received BS (2005) in Automation from Tianjin University, MSE (2009) in Software Engineering from Tsinghua University, and PhD (2013) in Computer Science from State University of New York at Buffalo. Currently I am an Associate Professor in the Computer Science and Technology Department at Nanjing University, a secretary of ACM Nanjing Chapter. My research interests include security, privacy, and economic incentives.
